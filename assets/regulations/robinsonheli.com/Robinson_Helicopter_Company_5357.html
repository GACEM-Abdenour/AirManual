COURSE ENROLLMENT
Please read the following before completing the enrollment form:
  • If paying with funds on account, please contact courses@robinsonheli.com to verify if funds on account will cover course fee before enrolling
  • Applications will not be processed without tuition.
  • Reservations and tuition is valid only for the pilot on confirmation letter and is not transferable.
  • Rescheduling must be received in writing at least 2 weeks of class date to avoid 50% penalty.
  • Cancellations received in writing at least 2 weeks of class date will incur 10% processing fee.
  • No refund will be issued for cancellations within 2 weeks of class date.
  • Refunds/credits will only be issued to original payment source.
  • “No shows” forfeit their tuition entirely.
