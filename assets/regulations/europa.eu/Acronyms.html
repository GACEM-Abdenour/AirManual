Acronyms

These terms are used throughout this guide and in the Safety Publications Tool application:


Acronym

Definition

AD

Airworthiness Directive, an AD which becomes effective 14 days after the date of issue

ADR

Airworthiness Directive Recommendation

 E AD

Emergency Airworthiness Directive, an AD which becomes effective two days after the date of issue

 P AD

Proposed Airworthiness Directive

 S IB

Safety Information Bulletin

 SD

Safety Directive

 P SD

Proposed Safety Directive
 

TC

Type Certificate
