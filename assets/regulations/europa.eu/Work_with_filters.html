Work with filters

    

If you are a registered user, you can have a list of filters to search the list of Safety Publications and get e-mail notificationswhen new items which match your filters are published. Filters make it convenient for you to search Safety Publications without having to specify the search criteria each time from scratch.

 

A filter is a combination of the form fields you will find in the advanced search page. It includes keywords, Publication types, starting and ending issue dates and the related TC holders/type designation, or the relevant Appliances, SIB or SD category.
