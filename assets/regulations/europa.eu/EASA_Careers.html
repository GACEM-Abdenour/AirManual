THE place to work. EASA.

EASA is the leading aviation authority in Europe. A career at EASA means contributing to our vision for an ever safer and greener civil aviation. As an EASA employee, you will be making a difference to the lives of about 500 million fellow Europeans: this is our shared purpose, and it could be yours, too. From our headquarters in Cologne, Germany, you will work with top experts in a multicultural work environment. Diversity and inclusion are part of our DNA and innovation is at the heart of what we do.

Spread your wings and fly with us!

The next step is yours. ​​​​​​Discover everything about EASA. In addition to core aviation profiles, we also look for talents in fields such as law, finance, HR, communications, IT, and many more. Whether you are looking for a new professional challenge, your first job, or a traineeship, here at EASA you can help shape the future of aviation!
