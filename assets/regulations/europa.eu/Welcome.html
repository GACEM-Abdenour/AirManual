Welcome to the Safety Publications Tool User's Guide.

The EASA Safety Publications Tool (SPT) is a web-based application developed to provide a searchable, printable list of: Airworthiness Directives (ADs), Safety Information Bulletins (SIBs) and Safety Directives (SD) published or endorsed by EASA and allow subscribed users to be automatically notified of new/amended publications.

issued/endorsed/adopted are now published within the EASA Safety Publication Tool.
