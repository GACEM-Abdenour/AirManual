Non-EU Member States Airworthiness Directives

All Foreign Airworthiness Directives adopted by EASA after the 15th of September 2008 under the provisions of EASA Decision 02/2003 of 14 October 2003 are now published within the EASA Safety Publication Tool and will be notified to the subscribed users.These notifications of Foreign ADs will be made in accordance with your filter settings same as for the receipt of EASA ADs.
