Research and Innovation

Research & Innovation and Artificial Intelligence (AI)

EASA’s strategic role in Research & Innovation is closely linked to European and global initiatives to ensure safe and sustainable aviation for air passengers and to keep European’s aviation industry ahead of the game. Our strategic goals, research projects and activities will help to shape the future of aviation in the key areas:

  • The European Green Deal & sustainability
  • Safety & security
  • Health in aviation
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
