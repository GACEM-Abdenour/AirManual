Contact Women and Gender Equality Canada

Women and Gender Equality Canada (WAGE) does not provide legal advice or legal assistance to individuals and organizations. Please contact a legal aid office, a local crisis centre or visit this webpage for assistance. For any emergency within Canada, please call 9-1-1.
