Programs

Transport Canada programs support our commitment to a safe and secure transportation system that’s environmentally responsible and innovative.

 Indigenous Participant Funding Program

The Indigenous Participant Funding Program (IPFP) funds projects and initiatives designed to support the capacity of Indigenous communities and organizations to participate in Canadian Navigable Waters Act (CNWA), the Wrecked, Abandoned or Hazardous Vessels Act (WAHVA) and the Federal Contaminated Sites Action Plan.

 Canada’s State Safety Program - International Civil Aviation Organization

Annual grant given to the ICAO to help other nations improve air safety and airworthiness within their borders.

Labrador Coastal Airstrips Restoration Program

Funding for restoring Labrador Coast airstrips to national certification standards.

A suite of programs administered by Transport Canada’s Aviation Security branch, designed to enhance the security of air cargo and inflight supplies throughout the supply chain.
