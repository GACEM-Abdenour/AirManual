Defence equipment

Defence equipment

The aircraft, ships, vehicles and weapons used by the Canadian Armed Forces (CAF) in training and operations. Investments in new equipment that will ensure that our military is ready to take on future challenges.

Most requested

  • Leopard 2A4 tank
  • C7A2 automatic rifle
  • C8A3 carbine automatic rifle
  • Light armoured vehicles (LAV) III
  • CC-177 Globemaster III
  • CH-147F Chinook

Services and information

Aircraft

The Royal Canadian Air Force’s fleet of aircraft including fighter jets, helicopters and training aircraft.

Weapons

The weapons used by CAF members during training and operations.

Vehicles

The suite of vehicles used by the Canadian Army, including armoured vehicles, tanks and carriers.

Fleet

The Royal Canadian Navy’s fleet of ships including warships, submarines and coastal defence vessels.

Defence equipment purchases and upgrades

Defence purchases, upgrades, and maintenance projects for land, sea, and air equipment.

Engineering support and testing

Regulations and test facilities that ensure the safety, security and effectiveness of military equipment.

Military airworthiness

The program, governing bodies and policy documents that regulate the safety of Canada’s military aircraft.
