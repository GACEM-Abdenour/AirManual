Prime Minister's Awards

From: Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada

The Prime Minister's Awards recognize teachers and early childhood educators for their achievements in education.

Award categories

Teaching Excellence

Recognizes elementary and secondary school teachers in all disciplines for their remarkable achievements in education and their commitment to preparing youth for a digital and innovation-based economy.

Teaching Excellence in STEM

Celebrates outstanding science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) teachers.

Early Childhood Education

Honours early childhood educators for their leadership, exemplary early childhood education practices and commitment to helping build the foundation children need to make the best possible start in life.
