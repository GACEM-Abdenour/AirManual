Publication inquiries

There are several ways you can order printed copies of Health Canada publications. To ensure 2-4 weeks delivery, provide your first and last name, full Canadian mailing address including postal code and a telephone number where you can be reached during the day, and the title of the publications and the quantities.

Order our publications and resources through our online ordering system.
