Contact Correctional Service Canada

Notice to readers:

There have been recent reports of fraudulent phone calls that appear to be from Correctional Service Canada. Correctional Service Canada did not place these calls. If you’ve received a suspicious phone call and wish to report it, contact the Canadian Anti-Fraud Centre, the agency responsible for dealing with fraudulent calls of this nature.
