Employment information

 

The Public Service Commission is unable to accept unsolicited requests for employment. Please visit the website listed below for employment opportunities within the federal public service.

  • GC Jobs page
  • Contact us form
  • Advertisements over the phone (jobs open to the public): 1-800-645-5605
  • General questions on how to apply online: 1-888-780-4444

Employment for students in the federal government

  • For questions concerning student employment programs with the federal government, call 1-888-780-4444
  • Federal Student Work Experience Program (FSWEP) email: cfp.pfete-fswep.psc@cfp-psc.gc.ca
  • Research Affiliate Program (RAP) email: cfp.par-rap.psc@cfp-psc.gc.ca
  • Post-secondary Co-operative Education and Internship Program (CO-OP/Internship) email: cfp.coop.psc@cfp-psc.gc.ca

Political activities

If you are interested in engaging in political activities, including seeking nomination as, or being, a candidate in a federal, provincial, territorial or municipal elections, you may contact your Designated Political Activities Representative or the PSC Political Activities and Non-Partisanship Directorate:

  • Email: cfp.activitespolitiques-politicalactivities.psc@cfp-psc.gc.ca
  • Phone: 819-420-8590 or 1-866-707-7152 (toll free)
  • Fax: 819-420-6546

You may obtain more information on the Political Activities Web site.
