Enterprise Vendor Engagement Centre

SSC engages with industry through CanadaBuys. If you have any questions about doing business with SSC, please send an email to our Enterprise Vendor Engagement Centre at enterprisevendorengagement-engagementdesfournisseurs@ssc-spc.gc.ca.

Support for Chief Information Officers

The Client Executive is the single point of contact for Chief Information Officers (CIO) for IT services offered to SSC partners and clients. CIOs looking for IT services support may contact the Client Executive responsible for their department or agency (accessible only on the Government of Canada network).
