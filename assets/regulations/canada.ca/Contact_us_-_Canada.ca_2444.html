Media relations

Library and Archives Canada (LAC) offers expertise across a wide range of topics. This includes items in our collections, such as Cabinet documents and military files, as well as our projects and initiatives, including Ādisōke and Project Naming, and our events and public programming.

For media inquiries, interview requests with our experts, or filming at LAC, journalists may contact our Media Relations team using the information below. To ensure a prompt response, we recommend including the following details in your email request:

  • the subject of your inquiry and any specific questions for LAC
  • the name of the media outlet you are writing for (or the intended outlet of publication if you are a freelance journalist)
  • your deadline or the date and time of publication or broadcast
