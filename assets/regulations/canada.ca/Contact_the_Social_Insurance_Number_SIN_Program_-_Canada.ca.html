I would like to apply, update, or obtain a SIN confirmation

You must submit a SIN application and provide all required documents to:

  • request a SIN for the first time
  • obtain a confirmation of your existing SIN
    • find out where you can locate your SIN without submitting an application
  • correct an error on a SIN record
  • update a legal change of name
  • update your status on your SIN record
  • update the expiry date of your immigration document on your SIN record
  • remove your disc number from your SIN record

The easiest way to apply, update or obtain a SIN confirmation is to apply online. If everything is in order, your SIN application will be processed within 5 business days.

You can also apply by mail or in person.

If you have questions, please visit the SIN program or contact us by phone.

Checking the status of a SIN application online

When did you apply?

  • 10 business days ago or less
  • More than 10 business days ago

Checking the status of a SIN application online 10 business days ago or less

You must wait 10 business days after it is submitted online before checking the status of the request.

If you provided your email address on your online application, you will receive 2 emails:

  • one confirming your application has been received
  • a second when your application has been processed

If your application does not meet the requirements, your second email will indicate what is required to reapply.

Checking the status of a SIN application online more than 10 business days ago

If it has been more than 10 days since you applied online, please consult the information below:

If you provided your email address, you will receive an email confirming your application has been received and a second email when your application has been processed.

If your application did not meet the requirements, you will receive an email or letter indicating that the application was rejected, as well as what is required to obtain a SIN.

If you choose to receive your SIN:

  • digitally and are eligible by signing in or registering for My Service Canada Account (MSCA) your SIN will be available to view and print your SIN as soon as your application is processed. You will not receive your SIN confirmation letter by mail.
  • by mail or you received an email indicating that you are not eligible to receive your SIN digitally, you will receive your SIN confirmation letter by mail.

If you do not receive your SIN or any other correspondence within the applicable timeframes, contact us by phone.

In Canada (toll-free): 1-866-274-6627
Canada TTY: 1-800-926-9105
Hours of operation: Monday to Friday from 8:30 am to 4:30 pm local time.
Closed statutory holidays.

I have questions about accessing my SIN through My Service Canada Account (MSCA)

You can view and print your Social Insurance Number (SIN) in My Service Canada Account (MSCA). Once registered or signed in to MSCA, you will be able to view and print your:

  • SIN
  • names as they appear on the SIN record
  • SIN expiry date (if applicable)

I am an employer and I need to confirm my employee’s SIN

As an employer, learn about your responsibilities related to your employees' SINs.

Your employee can view and print their SIN on My Service Canada Account (MSCA). For information on eligibility and how they can access their SIN visit My Service Canada Account.

Employers looking to confirm the SIN of a current or former employee must contact us by phone. You will need to provide:

  • your business number issued by Canada Revenue Agency, along with necessary information to verify the identity of your company, and;
  • information about the employee such as:
    • name (first given name, second given name, surname)
    • date of birth
    • SIN (if known)

If the employee's SIN is unknown, additional information is required, such as:

  • place of birth
  • SIN application number (SAN)

You can view and print your Social Insurance Number (SIN) in My Service Canada Account (MSCA). Once registered or signed in to MSCA, you will be able to view and print your:

  • SIN
  • names as they appear on the SIN record
  • SIN expiry date (if applicable)

I am an employer and I need to confirm my employee’s SIN

As an employer, learn about your responsibilities related to your employees' SINs.

Your employee can view and print their SIN on My Service Canada Account (MSCA). For information on eligibility and how they can access their SIN visit My Service Canada Account.

Employers looking to confirm the SIN of a current or former employee must contact us by phone. You will need to provide:

  • your business number issued by Canada Revenue Agency, along with necessary information to verify the identity of your company, and;
  • information about the employee such as:
    • name (first given name, second given name, surname)
    • date of birth
    • SIN (if known)

If the employee's SIN is unknown, additional information is required, such as:

  • place of birth
  • SIN application number (SAN)
