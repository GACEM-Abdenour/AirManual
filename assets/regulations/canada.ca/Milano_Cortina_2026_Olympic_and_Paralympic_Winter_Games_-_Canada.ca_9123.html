Milano Cortina 2026 Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games

Buongiorno Italia! In February and March 2026, over 250 athletes will proudly represent Canada at the Milano Cortina 2026 Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games in Italy.

These Games are a great opportunity for Canadian athletes to inspire the country, especially our youth, to stay active through sport. They embody determination, resilience, perseverance and excellence.

Behind every Canadian athlete is a network of support, and Canadians play a vital role in championing them. Sport Canada funding programs contribute to the preparation, participation, and celebration of our country’s athletic excellence. As the single largest investor in Canada's amateur sport system, the Government of Canada is committed to providing Canadian athletes with the resources and support they need to reach their full potential.

Go Canada!🍁
