Codes Canada publications

The National Model Codes and related user's guides are developed by the Canadian Board for Harmonized Construction Codes and published by the National Research Council of Canada (NRC). For the latest information about code changes, provisions or the code development system for the National Model Codes, please visit the Canadian Board for Harmonized Construction Codes website.

Access to codes and user's guides

You can access the National Model Codes, related user's guides and other codes publications for free in electronic format through the NRC Publications Archive.

To purchase printed copies:

  • Order online through the NRC Virtual Store, or
  • Send a completed order form (PDF, 264 KB) to Codes Canada Publication Sales by email or regular mail.

To find out which codes and regulations apply in your area, contact the appropriate provincial or territorial official.
