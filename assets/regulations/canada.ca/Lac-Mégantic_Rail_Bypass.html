About the bypass

The project is to build a 12.5 km rail bypass in Lac-Mégantic that will relocate the railway track away from downtown Lac-Mégantic.

Learn more about the bypass

Mitigation measures

Several mitigation measures are planned to minimize the potential impacts the project could have on the environment, the economy, and the community.

Learn more about mitigation measures

Canadian Transportation Agency Consultation on the Approval of Railway Line Construction

Before construction can begin, there are many regulatory steps that must be completed, including approval by the Canadian Transportation Agency.
