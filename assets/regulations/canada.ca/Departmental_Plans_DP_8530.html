Departmental Plans (DP)

Departmental Plans (DP) are part of the Government of Canada’s Estimates family of documents. Estimates documents support appropriation acts, which specify the amounts and broad purposes for which funds can be spent by the government. This page provides you with:

  • Links to all of the content within our current fiscal year’s DP, whose purpose is to inform Canadians about our Department’s plans for the current fiscal year; and
  • Historical records of Transport Canada's DPs since the 2006-2007 fiscal year.

We invite you to consult our Departmental Plan and we welcome your comments and questions about it.
