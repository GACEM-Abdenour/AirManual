Winterlude 2026

From January 30 to February 16, 2026, enjoy Canada’s Capital Region’s winter festival.

With its ice sculptures, snow slides, Ferris wheel, live music and many free outdoor winter activities, it’s the best way to enjoy the Canadian winter with family or friends. Experience a unique outdoor adventure in Ottawa and Gatineau.
