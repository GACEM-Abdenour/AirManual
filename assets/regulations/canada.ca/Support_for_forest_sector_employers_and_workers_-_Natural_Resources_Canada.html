Support for forest sector employers and workers

Need help navigating federal forest sector support? NRCan experts can provide guidance on which programs you or your business may be eligible for.

See below for more information on the measures available to support the forest sector and its workers. For expert advice to navigate available federal funding, you can submit a request form online.

 

On this page

  • Liquidity support
  • Transformation and diversification support
  • Worker support

The Government of Canada is protecting and strengthening sectors most affected by U.S. tariffs and is committed to helping workers gain new skills, supporting forest sector businesses as they modernize and diversify, and boosting demand for Canadian goods. Through this single-window pathfinding service and webpage, explore programs that provide liquidity support, assistance to workers affected by mill curtailments and closures, and programs to drive industry transformation.

Liquidity support

Financial support for mills and wood-product companies to manage cash flow pressure caused by softwood lumber duties, market volatility or tariff-related costs.

Loan guarantees

Softwood Lumber Guarantee Program

Loan guarantees for softwood lumber mills and manufacturers subject to U.S. softwood lumber duties

Direct loans and credit facilities

Pivot to Grow Loan

Loans for small and medium-sized companies facing uncertain export markets

Large Enterprise Tariff Loan facility

Financing support for large Canadian enterprises affected by actual and potential new tariffs and countermeasures

Export and trade financing

Trade Impact Program

Capital guarantees, trade credit insurance, and foreign exchange risk protection for export-focused companies

Transformation and market diversification support

Funding for businesses to innovate, diversify products and grow in new markets.

Research and development

Forest Innovation Program

Funds pre-commercial R&D to accelerate the development of products, processes and to support market penetration

Capital investments and construction

Investments in Forest Industry Transformation Program

Funds capital investments for the adoption of innovative processes and value-added forest sector products

Green Construction through Wood

Promotes wood-based building systems and products through demonstration projects, capacity building and standards

Strategic Response Fund

Supports large-scale transformative projects that build economic resilience, strengthen supply chains and protect jobs

Market diversification and growth

Global Forest Leadership Program

Supports forest sector international market diversification including market access through engagement and partnerships

Regional Tariff Response Initiative

Financial support for small and medium-sized forest sector companies affected by U.S. tariffs

Export Diversification Support

Insights, resources and opportunities to help you diversify your exports from the Trade Commissioner Service

Indigenous leadership

Indigenous Forestry Initiative

Supports greater Indigenous leadership, participation and business development in the forest sector

Worker support

Programs that provide training, income support and resources for affected workers and communities.

Upskilling and reskilling

Labour Market Agreements

Investment with provinces and territories supporting workers and employers with skills training

Skills and employment training for Indigenous people

Skill development and job training through Indigenous service organizations

Income and employment stability

EI Work-Sharing Program

Measures for employers experiencing a temporary reduction in business

Additional support measures announced

Federal Transportation Support: Funding to enable a 50% rate discount on interprovincial steel and lumber shipments within Canada.
