Privacy disclaimer

The personal information you include in an inquiry is protected under the Privacy Act and may be used to prepare a reply. Such information may also be used for statistical, evaluation and reporting purposes. The information is included in the personal information bank Public Communications (PSU 914). The information may be shared with programs and activities of FINTRAC should your inquiry pertain to a specific program or activity of the Centre. In such cases, the information is included in the personal information bank of the program or activity.

The transmission of this information is not secure so sensitive personal information, such as your Social Insurance Number, should not be included in your message.
