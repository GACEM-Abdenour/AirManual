Canada Strong Pass

This summer, we choose Canada! From June 19 to September 7, 2026, discover a wide range of experiences - from majestic parks and captivating museums, to inspiring historic sites and unforgettable train journeys. The Canada Strong Pass makes exploring our country more accessible than ever.

Start planning now and enjoy an affordable Canadian summer with free admission and reduced prices.

Free admission with Parks Canada

Explore national parks, national historic sites and national marine conservation areas.

Discounts on overnight stays with Parks Canada

Enjoy discounted rates on camping, roofed accommodations and historic stays.

Free or reduced admission at museums and galleries

Visit participating museums and galleries across the country. Visit the websites of each participating museum and gallery to find out about free admission and discounts for children and youth.

Free and discounted travel with VIA Rail

Children 17 and under travel free when accompanied by an adult. Passengers aged 18 to 24 benefit from discounted fares on travel across Canada. Conditions apply.

About the pass

The Canada Strong Pass is not a physical pass that you need to purchase, sign up for, or collect. Simply visit any participating Parks Canada site, museum, gallery, or VIA Rail to benefit from free admissions or discounts. Learn more about the pass eligibility details.
