Funding and trade support for Canadian exporters

Access government programs, expert guidance and trade resources that help businesses enter international markets, manage global risk and take advantage of cross-border opportunities.

Canada–United States: Supports and information

Get information on services and supports for businesses and workers involved in Canada–U.S. trade. Find guidance on tariffs, CUSMA compliance, travel requirements, financial support and sector specific programs that help companies operate and expand across the border.

Canadian Commercial Corporation (CCC)

CCC helps Canadian businesses secure international government contracts. It advocates for proposals, offers expert guidance and reduces risk to help companies succeed in government markets worldwide.

Export Development Canada (EDC)

EDC provides financial solutions, advisory services, free trade insights and resources for businesses looking to take on their first international contract or expand global operations. EDC’s programs help companies manage international risk and strengthen their readiness for global trade.

Trade Commissioner Service (TCS)

The Trade Commissioner Service provides export advisory services, introductions to qualified contacts, funding, accelerator programs, and tailored support at trade events. With a presence in more than 160 cities worldwide, the TCS can help you do business with the world.
