Archived Weekly Checklist issues

The online Weekly Checklist is available in three formats:

1. Full Checklist
The Full Checklist is the complete edition of the Weekly Checklist of Canadian Government Publications.
2. Electronic Items
Electronic Items offers a filtered view of the Full Checklist where only online publications are displayed. This format provides quick access to new items that have been added to our E-collection as well as to new online publications at other Government of Canada Web sites.
3. MARC 21 Format
The MARC 21 Format provides access to a download file that contains machine readable MARC 21 batch records for all new monograph and serial titles included in the Full Checklist.
