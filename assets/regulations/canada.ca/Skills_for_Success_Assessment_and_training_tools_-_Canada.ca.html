Skills for Success – Assessment and training tools

Find the right tools to assess or upgrade your skills. Many of these tools are free to use. New tools will be added regularly as they are developed.

If you are an employer, training provider or career counsellor looking for tools to support your clients or learners, set the “Target Audience” filter below, to “employer ” or “employment/training service provider” instead of individual. Some of these tools have a cost for you to use them, as noted in the description.

The tools listed are from the Government of Canada and outside organizations. Organizations that wish to have their tool(s) included, may view the criteria below to apply.

For additional information on the skills, visit the Learn about the Skills page.

To post your training or assessment tool on our website

If your organization developed a tool or tools that you would like to have featured on the website, request an application form. Prior to contacting the SFS Program, please ensure that the tool or inventory meets the required criteria below:

Criteria for training and assessment tools

The tool:

  • addresses at least 1 of the 9 Skills for Success, and
  • is or was funded by Canadian federal, provincial or territorial government(s), and
  • was launched within the past 2 years, or
  • has been downloaded or visited at least 100 times within the past year

Criteria for tool inventories

The inventory:

  • addresses at least 1 of the 9 Skills for Success
  • is populated with information that has been validated by the applicant organization
