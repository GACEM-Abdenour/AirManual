Health Emergency Readiness Canada

Health Emergency Readiness Canada (HERC) is a special operating agency within Innovation, Science, and Economic Development Canada (ISED). Its focus is to drive innovation and boost Canada's industrial capacity to advance leading-edge technologies and support the growth of the life sciences sector in Canada.

HERC works closely with federal partners, including Health Canada and the Public Health Agency of Canada, to support the development and production of key medical countermeasures (MCMs) to protect Canadians in the event of a health emergency.
