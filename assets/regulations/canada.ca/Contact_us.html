Contact us

At FedNor, we care about your comments and suggestions. A variety of contact tools have been put in place to make it easier for you get in touch with us, depending on the subject matter you wish to address.

To talk about your business project

Select your program delivery region to find the officer in your region, for general inquiries contact a regional manager or access the contact information for all our business offices.

To make a request under the Access to Information Act or the Privacy Act

To learn how to make a request, see the terms and conditions web page.

To find an employee's contact information

Contact information for all FedNor employees can be found in the Government Electronic Directory Services (GEDS), a directory of federal public servants for all regions across Canada.

Media enquiries

For information, media representatives can send an e-mail to media@fednor.gc.ca.
