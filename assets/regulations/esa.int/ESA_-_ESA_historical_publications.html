History study reports

The ESA History Study Reports started as preliminary reports of studies carried out to form the basis of a comprehensive study of European space activities from 1959–87, eventually published as ESA SP-1235.
The series continued with issues covering space activities in each of our Member States.

Monographs

The SP-xxxx series of monographs (known as 4-digit SPs) consists primarily of scientific and technical books giving detailed information on ESA’s missions, activities and interests. They are authored by highly-respected experts in their fields. The series also covers other substantial content such as historical books.

  • SP-1337: ESA Convention and Council Rules of Procedure
  • SP-1329 vol.II: ESA's Living Planet Programme: Scientific Achievements and Future Challenges
  • SP-1326: ERS Missions: 20 Years of Observing Earth

  • SP-1261 Switzerland, Europe and Space – Adventure and Imperative
  • SP-1248: Sweden in Space
