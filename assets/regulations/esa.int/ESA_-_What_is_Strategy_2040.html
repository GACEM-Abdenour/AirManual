ESA Council has finalised Strategy 2040. The new strategy will guide the European Space Agency’s work including protecting our planet and climate, exploration and discovery, enhancing Europe’s autonomy and resilience, helping to strengthen Europe as a commercial space hub, and inspiring the coming generations of Europeans.
