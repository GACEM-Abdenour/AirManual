Welcome to the H2020 HSNAV homepage.

Under a delegation agreement by the European Commission, the European Space Agency has undertaken the technical and management implementation of the Horizon 2020 Framework Programme for Research and Innovation in Satellite Navigation.

Further information on space research in Horizon 2020 can be found at the DG-DEFIS webpage.
